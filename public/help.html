Introduction

    This is a team game, there will be 2 teams and each team will consists of 2 players.
    From a standard deck of 52 cards 32 cards are taken. 7, 8, 9, 10, J, Q, K & A from each color suit(Spades, Hearts, Clubs, Diamonds).The aim of the game is to win tricks containing valuable cards. The values of the cards are according to priority:
    J - 3 points
    9 - 2 points
    A - 1 points
    10 - 1 points
    K - no points
    Q - no points
    8 - no points
    7 - no points
    This gives a total of 28 points for cards. In some versions of the game, the last trick is worth an extra card point, for a total of 29: this total explains the name of the game. Most players nowadays do not count the point for the last trick, but the name of the game is still 29, even when playing this version with only 28 points.
    Traditionally, the Twos, Threes Fours and Fives discarded from the full 52-card pack are used as trump indicators: each player takes a set of these cards, one of each suit. The Sixes are used to keep score: each partnership uses one red and one black Six for this purpose.

Rules

    Bids starts from 16 and goes upto maximum of 28.
    If the Pair is declared by the bidder or his partner, the effect is to reduce by 4 the number of card points they require to fulfil their bid, subject to a minimum of 16; if an opponent of the bidder declares a Pair, it increases the number of points required by the bidding side by 4, subject to a maximum of 28.
    If any player bids, the auction continues for as many rounds as necessary until three players pass in succession. If the first three players pass, the dealer is forced to bid 16, which ends the auction.
    If trumps have not been declared by the end of the play, the deal is annulled, and neither team scores.
    The game is cancelled if any of the following things happen.
    If any player possesses four jacks.
    If all 8 cards are pointless
    If the person next to the shuffler has pointless cards
    If the opposite pair playing against the bidding pair do not have any of the 8 trump cards

Insructions to play

    The game will start when any player will click on the deal botton.
    Each player will get 4 cards to bid according to their expectation.
    The bidding will take place between 2 players at a time.
    The highest bidder will get chance to chose trump suit.
    Each player will get another 4 cards.
    The player who's card background will be highlighted, will start playing.
